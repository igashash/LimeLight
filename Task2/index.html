Beyond The Stars

Explore The Universe

Humanity has always searched beyond the horizon, looking toward the stars for discovery and adventure. Across endless galaxies and drifting planets, the universe continues to inspire imagination.

This parallax experience recreates the feeling of floating through deep space using layered animation, scrolling effects, and immersive visuals that move at different speeds.

Massive planets drift slowly through the darkness while distant stars remain frozen in the background. The astronaut moves gently across the screen creating the illusion of depth and movement.
